**Memory Crisis: Sony Halts Global Memory Card Sales Amid Supply Shortages**

Sony has taken the drastic measure of temporarily suspending sales of its memory card products due to supply shortages. As of March 27th, 2026, the company will no longer accept orders for nearly all CFexpress and SD memory cards, including popular models such as CFexpress Type A, Type B, and high-capacity SDXC/SDHC cards. While a few lower-end Type B models and select SF-UZ series products will continue to be available, customers will no longer be able to purchase the majority of Sony’s memory card offerings.
