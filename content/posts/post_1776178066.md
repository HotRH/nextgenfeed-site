The release of the iPhone 17 series has sparked a frenzy of case purchasing, as users seek to protect their new devices from the perils of daily use. However, with the introduction of new features such as MagSafe and the Camera Plateau, the landscape of iPhone cases has undergone significant changes. In this article, we delve into the world of iPhone 17 cases and accessories, providing a comprehensive guide for users to navigate the vast array of options available.

The Need for Cases

Despite the Ceramic Shield 2 glass on the front of the iPhone 17 being touted as three times more scratch-resistant than its predecessors, the reality is that glass is still glass. Scratches can occur, and it's essential to have a reliable case to keep your phone in pristine condition. With over 100 cases tested, we've identified the best options for users to consider.

The Exception: iPhone 17e

One of the primary challenges when upgrading from an older iPhone model is finding a new case that fits. Unfortunately, most older iPhone cases will not fit the iPhone 17 lineup, with one notable exception - the iPhone 17e. Users can reuse their old iPhone 16e cases, but it's crucial to consider MagSafe compatibility if you have a MagSafe-enabled iPhone.

Camera Plateau and Camera Control

Two new features introduced in the iPhone 17 series are the Camera Plateau and Camera Control. The former refers to the raised camera module on the back of the iPhone 17 Pro and Pro Max, while the latter is a button that allows users to access the camera app, take photos, or capture video. Case manufacturers have responded by creating cases with cutouts for this button, offering a symmetrical design.

MagSafe: A Necessity

All recommended cases in our guide feature MagSafe technology, which uses magnets to ensure magnetic accessories remain attached. It's essential to explore the world of MagSafe accessories, as there are many options available. Our guides on Apple's 3-in-1 chargers, MagSafe wallets, and MagSafe accessories provide more information.

Choosing the Right Case

To select the right case for your iPhone 17, it's crucial to consider the size of your device. The handy guide provided above helps users identify their phone model based on screen size. Additionally, note that cases designed for the iPhone 17 and Pro models are not interchangeable due to differences in dimensions.

Scratchgate: A Growing Concern

The issue of scratches affecting iPhone 17 models has garnered significant attention online. Apple's use of anodized aluminum on the redesigned Pro and Pro Max models has led to a higher likelihood of scratches appearing around the Camera Plateau module. To mitigate this problem, users can opt for cases that cover this area rather than leaving it exposed.

Testing Cases

While we cannot conduct drop tests due to limitations in our testing environment, we do thoroughly inspect every case installed onto the latest iPhones. Our top pick, the Native Union Active Case, is a great example of a case that covers the Camera Plateau and provides excellent protection for your device.

In conclusion, selecting the right iPhone 17 case requires careful consideration of several factors, including MagSafe compatibility, camera cutouts, and scratch resistance. By understanding the unique features of the iPhone 17 series and the various options available, users can make an informed decision about which case best suits their needs.
