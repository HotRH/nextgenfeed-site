In a move to revolutionize the fast food industry, Dairy Queen has announced the widespread implementation of an AI chatbot in its drive-thrus across the United States and Canada. This innovative technology aims to streamline orders and encourage customers to increase their purchases, a strategy that has been gaining traction among fast food chains in recent years.

The AI-powered drive-thru system, developed by Presto, is designed to accurately take orders approximately 90% of the time, according to reports. While human workers may still be involved in certain aspects of the process, as revealed by Bloomberg in 2023, the chatbot's primary function is to interact with customers and facilitate efficient transactions. The system has already undergone testing at Dairy Queen, which showed promising results during a free ice cream promotion event.

The incorporation of AI-powered drive-thrus is not a new concept for fast food chains. Wendy's, for instance, began experimenting with Google-powered AI drive-thrus in 2023, while McDonald's briefly piloted a chatbot-powered drive-thru as well. Taco Bell has also tested its AI drive-thrus, although the experience was met with some customer frustration and attempts to troll the system. Burger King is taking a more nuanced approach by testing the technology in select restaurants and using it to measure employee "friendliness" and assist with meal preparation.

Dairy Queen's decision to roll out this technology across multiple locations reflects its commitment to staying ahead of the curve in the fast-paced world of fast food. By leveraging AI-powered drive-thrus, the company is poised to improve customer satisfaction, increase efficiency, and potentially boost sales. The lack of specific location details for the rollout only adds to the excitement surrounding this innovative move.

Ultimately, Dairy Queen's adoption of AI-powered drive-thrus marks a significant step forward in the evolution of the fast food industry. As consumers increasingly demand seamless, tech-enabled experiences, it is imperative that fast food chains adapt and innovate to meet these expectations. With Presto's AI chatbot at the helm, Dairy Queen is well-positioned to capitalize on this trend and revolutionize the way customers interact with its drive-thrus.
