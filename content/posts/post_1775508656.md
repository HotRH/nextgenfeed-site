The Netflix Playground app, which can be accessed offline without requiring a mobile or Wi-Fi connection, is designed to provide young users with a seamless and engaging experience. The app's launch titles include interactive games such as "Playtime With Peppa Pig," where players jump into Peppa's world for playful activities, and "Sesame Street," which features memory card matching and connect-the-dots coordination exercises.
