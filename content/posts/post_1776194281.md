As the tech world continues to grapple with the complexities of artificial intelligence (AI), venture capitalists, founders, and innovators are converging on San Francisco for the first StrictlyVC event of the year. Scheduled for April 30th at the Sentro Filipino Cultural Center, this highly anticipated gathering promises to deliver insights into obtaining funding, AI innovations, and the latest trends in startup investing.

Corporate VCs: The Unlikely Leaders

One of the most intriguing aspects of this event is the appearance of Nicolas Sauvage, President of TDK Ventures. With a $500 million investment fund dedicated to early-stage startups, Sauvage will share his expertise on what drives corporate venture capital (VC) decisions and offer valuable advice for founders looking to secure funding from these investors. As the leader of TDK's investment efforts, Sauvage has steered the company into backing 52 startups and three unicorns, making him an ideal speaker for startup enthusiasts.

AI Trustworthiness: The Future of Information

In a world where AI-powered platforms are increasingly relied upon for advice and information, ensuring trustworthiness is crucial. Campbell Brown, former CNN host and current co-founder and CEO of Forum AI, will join the conversation to discuss how to vet, verify, and sustain the veracity of information provided by large language models (LLMs). With her background in journalism and her experience leading a team focused on making AI platforms more trustworthy, Brown's insights are sure to be valuable for anyone invested in the future of AI-driven communication.

The Rise of Replit: A Code Revolution

Amjad Masad, co-founder and CEO of Replit, will take the stage to share his firsthand experience helping lead a revolution in how software is constructed. The rise of vibe coding has changed the way many developers work, especially in the Bay Area, and brought potent competitors like Anthropic and OpenAI into the mix. Attendees will gain valuable insights into the future of programming as Masad discusses his company's role in shaping this new landscape.

Convergence and Connection

This event promises to be a unique opportunity for attendees to connect with some of the greatest minds and most well-connected members of the startup community. With a stacked speaker lineup, attendees can expect to gain valuable insights into AI innovations, corporate VC strategies, and the latest trends in startup investing. As the event concludes, attendees will have ample time to network and make meaningful connections that can help drive their own projects forward.
