As the technology landscape continues to evolve, Google's AI Mode has emerged as a game-changer in the world of artificial intelligence. The latest update brings not only a redesigned interface but also expanded capabilities that cater to users worldwide. In this article, we'll delve into the details of this significant development and explore its implications for the future of AI-driven applications.

The revamped prompt box, reminiscent of Google's Gemini app, offers a sleeker and more user-friendly experience. Gone is the traditional pop-up menu on mobile devices, replaced by a bottom sheet that provides easy access to key features. The "Tools" section has been streamlined, with "Create images" being the only option available on mobile devices at present. The "Gemini 3 models" switcher remains intact, allowing users to toggle between Auto and Pro modes.

The new design is currently rolling out to both Android and iOS devices, ensuring a consistent experience across platforms. While the updated interface may seem like a minor tweak, it sets the stage for future innovations that will further solidify AI Mode's position as a cutting-edge tool.

Beyond the visual overhaul, Google has announced the global expansion of AI Mode's agentic booking capabilities. This feature, which can book restaurants on behalf of users, is no longer limited to the US market. The updated service now caters to eight new markets: Australia, Canada, Hong Kong, India, New Zealand, Singapore, South Africa, and the UK.

To use this feature, users simply need to specify their preferences in a prompt, including time, location, cuisine, party size, or desired ambiance. AI Mode will then scour multiple reservation platforms and websites, leveraging local partnerships to find suitable options. The possibilities are endless, from booking a table for two at a dog-friendly Italian restaurant in Shoreditch on Saturday evening to finding a sushi spot nearby that serves vegan tempura.

This global expansion marks a significant milestone for Google's AI Mode, demonstrating the company's commitment to making this technology accessible to a wider audience. As we move forward into an era where AI-driven applications continue to transform our daily lives, the possibilities are endless, and AI Mode is poised to play a leading role in shaping this future.
