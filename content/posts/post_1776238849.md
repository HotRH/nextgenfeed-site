The latest offering from Roborock, the Saros 20, has solidified its position as one of the best robot vacuums on the market. Building upon years of experience and refinement, this new iteration focuses on enhanced navigation, making it a must-have for those seeking to upgrade their cleaning experience.

At the heart of the Saros 20 is its impressive suction power, boasting an increase of over 60% to a whopping 36,000 Pa. This significant boost allows the vacuum to effortlessly pull dog hair off carpeted areas, a feat that has long been a source of frustration for many users. The mop, too, has seen improvements with the introduction of dual heads, providing a more thorough cleaning experience.

One of the most notable advancements in the Saros 20 is its upgraded "AdaptiLift Chassis." This innovative system enables the robot to effortlessly traverse thresholds up to 3.46 inches high, effectively eliminating the risk of getting stuck or damaged. In my own testing, I've witnessed this feature in action, as the Saros 20 navigated complex floor transitions with ease, a significant improvement over its predecessor.

Another welcome upgrade is the redesigned "RockDock," which now boasts a sleek, matte grey exterior and improved internals for hot water cleaning and faster mop drying. This revamped dock has already shown promising results, retaining its cleanliness and functionality better than previous models.

The Roborock app remains an intuitive and user-friendly companion to the Saros 20, offering a comprehensive map and convenient features at your fingertips. The addition of AI-driven "Pet-Friendly Intelligence" is also a welcome feature, allowing users to capture and track their pets' movements within the home – though I've found that most pets, including my own, are more intimidated by robot vacuums than anything else.

What sets the Saros 20 apart from its predecessors is its focus on refinement rather than reinvention. By addressing long-standing pain points, such as threshold crossing and improved navigation, Roborock has created a product that is truly greater than the sum of its parts.

For those who have not upgraded their robot vacuum in recent years, the Saros 20 is an excellent choice, offering a significant improvement over previous models. With its impressive suction power, advanced navigation, and refined features, this robot vacuum is poised to revolutionize the way we clean our homes.
