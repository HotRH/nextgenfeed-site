title: "NASA Launches Four Astrists Toward the Moon on the Artemis II Mission"
date: 2026-04-02T09:51:27Z
draft: false
---

NASA's highly anticipated Artemis II flight has reached orbit, successfully propelling four astronauts toward the Moon for the first time in more than 50 years. The mission marks a major milestone in NASA's Artemis program, aimed at returning humans to the lunar surface by as early as 2028. On Wednesday evening, the spacecraft carrying the astronauts lifted off from Kennedy Space Center's Launch Complex 39A and entered Earth's orbit before beginning its journey to the Moon. The four astronauts on board will spend several days in transit, during which time they will conduct various scientific experiments and tests to prepare for their eventual descent to the lunar surface.
