In recent months, the tech industry has witnessed a flurry of budget-friendly phone launches, but with no major releases expected until next year, the time is ripe for a reassessment of the best affordable options available today. As we dive into the world of budget phones, it's clear that the market has become increasingly competitive, with manufacturers vying for attention and consumer dollars.

At the forefront of our recommendations is Apple's iPhone 17E, which takes its rightful place as the top pick for cheap iPhones. While not a surprise to anyone, this year's addition of MagSafe charging has significantly enhanced the overall package, making it an attractive option for those seeking performance without breaking the bank. The A19 chip, shared with the flagship iPhone 17, ensures that you won't find a phone with better processing power at this price point.

On the Android side, Google's Pixel 10A may not have revolutionized the market with its lack of innovation, but last year's Pixel 9A remains a strong contender. The carbon copy of this device still offers excellent software support and a capable camera, making it the top choice for most users seeking an affordable Android option.

However, competition is indeed on the rise, with Nothing's Phone 4A Pro emerging as a compelling alternative. This larger-than-average phone boasts a stunning display and an original aesthetic that sets it apart from its competitors. What's more, unlike previous Nothing phones, this device is now available for purchase in the US, making it an attractive option for those seeking a unique Android experience.

Ultimately, when it comes to budget-friendly phones under $600, there are plenty of options to consider. Whether you're drawn to Apple's iPhone 17E or Google's Pixel 9A, or perhaps something entirely different like Nothing's Phone 4A Pro, the key takeaway is that you don't have to sacrifice performance for affordability. With these top picks on your radar, you can rest assured that there's a budget-friendly phone out there that will meet – and exceed – your expectations without breaking the bank.
