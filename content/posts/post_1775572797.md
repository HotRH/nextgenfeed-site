In a move that is set to revolutionize podcast discovery, Spotify has announced that its Prompted Playlist feature will now be available for podcasts as well. This AI-powered feature, which was initially rolled out in New Zealand and later expanded to other markets, allows users to create personalized playlists by providing a prompt or theme. With the addition of podcasts to this feature, listeners can now discover new shows and episodes that cater specifically to their interests.

The Prompted Playlist feature is designed to make discovery effortless and personal. Users can provide a prompt such as "Make a playlist of True Crime podcasts I would be interested in" and the AI will curate a list of relevant episodes from Spotify's vast library. The feature also allows users to customize the frequency at which their playlists are updated, with options including daily, weekly (with a selection of days), and no updates.

The impact of this feature on podcast discovery cannot be overstated. With over 34 million podcasts discovered for the first time every week on Spotify, the platform is already a hub for podcast enthusiasts. The addition of Prompted Playlists will make it even easier for listeners to find new shows that align with their interests and preferences. For creators, this feature unlocks powerful opportunities for discovery, bringing both back catalog and new episodes to audiences who are actively signaling what they want to hear.

Spotify's efforts to enhance podcast discovery are not limited to the Prompted Playlist feature. The company has also made significant changes to its platform to make it more podcast-friendly. Earlier this year, Spotify lowered the criteria for monetization for video podcasts, making it easier for creators to earn revenue from their shows. This move is a testament to the growing importance of podcasts in Spotify's overall strategy.

As Spotify continues to evolve and improve its platform, it is clear that the company is committed to providing users with an unparalleled listening experience. The addition of Prompted Playlists for podcasts is just the latest example of this commitment, and it will be fascinating to see how this feature shapes the future of podcast discovery on the platform.
