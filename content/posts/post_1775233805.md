As the music world prepares for one of its most iconic events, YouTube has announced that it will be streaming the Coachella music festival from April 10 onwards. This year's live stream promises to be more immersive than ever, featuring 4K streams, multiview support, and exclusive content captured on Google Pixel devices.

For fans who can't attend the festival in person, YouTube is offering a comprehensive live stream experience that will allow viewers to feel like they're right there at the Coachella grounds. The platform has confirmed that it will be broadcasting from seven different stages, including both horizontal and vertical streams from the Quasar stage. What's more, the vertical streams will be shot exclusively on Google Pixel devices, providing a unique perspective on the action.

One of the most exciting new features this year is YouTube Shorts' dedicated vertical livestream, which will offer exclusive angles and footage filmed entirely on Pixel devices. This will give viewers a chance to experience Coachella like never before, with a behind-the-scenes look at the festival's most iconic moments.

In addition to these innovative features, Coachella fans will also be able to enjoy 4K streams from three main stages – the Coachella Stage, Outdoor Theatre, and Sahara. This level of visual fidelity is sure to provide an unparalleled viewing experience for those who can't make it to the festival in person.

But that's not all – YouTube has also announced the return of multiview support for TVs. For fans who want to experience multiple stages at once, this feature will allow them to stream up to four stages simultaneously and effortlessly toggle between audio feeds. This means that viewers won't have to miss out on their favorite sets or performances.

The Coachella live stream experience wouldn't be complete without some unique features to enhance the viewing experience. YouTube has announced a dedicated Coachella livestream app for Android and iOS, which will allow fans to plan their schedule with Gemini's help and stay up-to-date with the latest festival news and highlights. Exclusive merch drops from popular artists will also be available via YouTube Shopping, giving fans the chance to own a piece of the Coachella magic.

To make the experience even more engaging, YouTube is launching its "Watch With" series, featuring popular YouTubers who will be hosting live streams and providing commentary on their favorite Coachella performances. This is sure to add an extra layer of excitement and community to the viewing experience.

In conclusion, this year's Coachella live stream on YouTube promises to be a game-changer for music fans around the world. With its innovative features, exclusive content, and enhanced viewing experience, the platform is set to revolutionize the way we consume music festivals online. Don't miss out – tune in from April 10 onwards to experience Coachella like never before!
