The world of home entertainment is on the cusp of a significant upgrade, courtesy of Walmart's latest move to refresh its Onn 4K Pro streaming box. The e-commerce giant has quietly listed an updated version of the device, which promises to revolutionize the way we consume our favorite shows and movies.

One of the most notable enhancements in the new Onn 4K Pro is the integration of Google Gemini technology. This means that users will be able to enjoy seamless hands-free voice control, searching, and recommendations, courtesy of Google's upgraded assistant. This feature has the potential to significantly enhance the viewing experience, making it easier than ever to find something to watch.

Another significant update in the new Onn 4K Pro is its compatibility with Matter-over-Thread (Matter) protocol. This development is a major milestone for Walmart, as it marks the company's official entry into the Connectivity Standards Alliance, which developed the Matter protocol. The implications of this move are far-reaching, as it opens up new possibilities for seamless device control and smart home integration.

The updated Onn 4K Pro also boasts improved hardware specifications, including a larger design that bears a striking resemblance to Google TV Streamer 4K. While the new device is more expensive than its predecessor at $59.88, it still offers excellent value for money, considering its enhanced features and capabilities.

In terms of performance, the new Onn 4K Pro promises to deliver smooth streaming experiences, thanks to its 3GB of RAM, 32GB of storage, and support for 4K Ultra UHD and Dolby Vision. The device also features both Wi-Fi 6 and ethernet port connectivity, ensuring that users can enjoy high-speed streaming without any hiccups.

While some may argue that the new Onn 4K Pro is not a revolutionary upgrade, its integration of Google Gemini technology and Matter protocol compatibility make it a significant step forward in terms of smart home control and voice-assisted entertainment. As Walmart continues to evolve its offerings in the world of streaming and smart home devices, consumers can expect even more exciting developments on the horizon.
